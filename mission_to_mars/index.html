About Our Mission To Reach Mars

Mission, Obsession, Destiny?

Why go to mars? It's a barren, hostile environment in which life simply cannot survive. Making the planet hospitable will require some serious ingenuity and resources if it can be achieved. Why is this our mission? Is it possible by spreading to other planets, just as we have spread across the Earth, we increase our chances of survival of the human race? Maybe it's really cool to travel to distant places, and the moon was just the beginning of our future history of space travel. Perhaps it is our destiny. After all, the universe began in a much simpler state then it is in now, and with the powerful forces at work creating the building blocks of life, starting with the powerful processes of the stars, we are destined to return to our place among the stars. After all, we are made up of ancient atoms from stars many many years ago...
